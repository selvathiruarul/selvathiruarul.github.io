Crime hot spot analysis

Crime Hot spots are areas on a map that have high crime intensity. They are developed for researchers and analysts for crime examination in geographic locations. Developing maps that contain hotspots are becoming a critical and influential tool for policing, they help to develop knowledge and understanding of different places where crime occurrences are frequent. This knowledge can help public to choose their places based on how safe it is.
